Did you know these?

At the age of seven, she decided to determine how an alarm clock worked and dismantled seven alarm clocks before her mother realized what she was doing.

Though Hopper invented a foundation of English based programming language, her Latin score at school made her rejected for an early admission to a college. She was admitted the following year.

Her initial application to enlist in the Navy was refused due partly to her age and weight.
