Hometown

John von Neumann was born in Budapest, Hungary on December 28, 1903. He grew up in a wealthy, well-educated family and showed an early aptitude for mathematics.

Budapest was an intellectual and cultural hub during von Neumann's early life, and he was exposed to many great minds in various fields. He attended the Lutheran Gymnasium in Budapest, where he was one of the top students in his class.

Von Neumann's childhood home still stands in Budapest and is now a museum dedicated to his life and work.
