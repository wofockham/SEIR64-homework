Hometown for the great Jenn Schiffer

Where Jenn grew up

  • Jersey City is the second-largest city in New Jersey, with a population of around 270,000.
  • The city covers an area of 21.1 square miles and is situated on the western bank of the Hudson River, opposite Manhattan.
  • Jersey City was originally founded by the Dutch in 1630 and was later settled by the British in the 17th century.
  • The city has a rich history as a major transportation hub, and it's home to several historic railroad terminals and freight yards.
  • Jersey City has a diverse population, with large African American, Hispanic, and Asian communities.
