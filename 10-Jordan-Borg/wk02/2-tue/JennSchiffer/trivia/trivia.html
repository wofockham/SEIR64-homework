Trivia Time!! Did you know this about Jenn??!?!.

  • 1. Jenn Schiffer is a software engineer, artist, writer, and educator now based in New York.
  • 2. She was the Community Engineer at Fog Creek Software and a Developer Advocate at Microsoft.
  • 3. Schiffer is the creator of Make8BitArt.com, a tool for creating pixel art.
  • 4. She has a background in performance art and studied theatre at Montclair State University.
  • 5. Schiffer has written for numerous publications, including The New Yorker, Vice, and Wired.
  • 6. In 2017, she co-founded Tech Under Thirty, a community for young people in tech.
  • 7. Schiffer has been featured in The New York Times, Forbes, and Fast Company.
  • 8. She is known for her humorous and irreverent approach to tech culture and has been described as a 'tech satirist.'
  • 9. Schiffer is also a prolific Twitter user and has over 38,000 followers.
  • 10. In her spare time, she enjoys drawing, playing video games, and practicing mindfulness meditation.
