Jenn Schiffer

Jenn Schiffer is a web developer, artist, and writer based in Jersey City, New Jersey.

She is a front-end developer advocate at Microsoft, and she is known for her contributions to many open source projects such as jQuery and Bower.

In her free time, she enjoys making art and doing stand-up comedy.
