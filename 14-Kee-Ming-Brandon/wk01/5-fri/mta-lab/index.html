There are 3 subway lines:

The N line stops: Times Square, 34th, 28th, 23rd, Union Square, and 8th

The L line stops: 8th, 6th, Union Square, 3rd, and 1st

The 6 line stops: Grand Central, 33rd, 28th, 23rd, Union Square, and Astor Place.

All 3 subway lines intersect at Union Square, but there are no other intersection points.
